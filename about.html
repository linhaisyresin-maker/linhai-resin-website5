OUR HISTORY

Jiangsu Linhai Resin Science And Technology Co., Ltd. was founded in 1972. Originally established as a state-owned chemical plant, we successfully restructured into a private enterprise in 2001.

Today, we are proud to serve as a director unit of the China Ion Exchange Resin Industry Association. With decades of continuous innovation, we bridge the gap between complex polymer chemistry and large-scale industrial applications globally.

Corporate Mission
