CERTIFICATIONS & QUALITY ASSURANCE

More than just documents—our certifications reflect a deeply ingrained culture of rigorous quality control, environmental stewardship, and industrial safety.

OUR QUALITY MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

A closed-loop quality control process ensures atomic-level precision from raw material intake to final product delivery.

01. Raw Material Inspection

Strict purity checks on monomers and cross-linking agents before they enter our facility. We source only premium-grade styrene and DVB.

02. In-Process Monitoring

Real-time control of polymerization temperature and cross-linking density to ensure optimal porosity and mechanical stability of the resin matrix.

03. Final Lab Testing

Every batch undergoes exhaustive testing for exchange capacity, moisture retention, true density, and sphericity before receiving a COA.
