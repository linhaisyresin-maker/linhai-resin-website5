RIGOROUS Q.C. PROTOCOLS

We do not rely on estimates. Every batch of Linhai Resin undergoes exhaustive physical and chemical testing to ensure it meets our published specifications and performs flawlessly under extreme industrial conditions.

Moisture Content Precision

Moisture retention dictates exchange capacity. We utilize advanced 2600 r/min centrifugation (6-minute cycles) coupled with high-precision analytical balances to measure absolute moisture percentages.

Sphericity & Attrition Testing

Mechanical strength is critical. Resins are subjected to intense mechanical stress in a specialized Silicon Nitride Ball Mill (125 r/min). We guarantee a post-attrition sphericity rate of ≥90% for most models.

True & Apparent Density

Using precision densitometers, we calculate exact wet true densities (excluding interstitial voids) to ensure perfect bed stratification for double-layer and mixed-bed systems.

Testing Capacity

Over 50 distinct titration and spectroscopic analyses performed daily.
