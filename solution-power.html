Solution Spotlight: LH-836EH

Revolutionary Acid Removal for Fire-Resistant (EH) Oil

In thermal power plants, the degradation of phosphate ester fire-resistant oil (EH Oil) leads to increased acidity, causing servo valve sticking and system failure. Traditional media filters are insufficient.

500% Efficiency Increase

The LH-836EH specialized matrix offers an acid absorption efficiency over 5x higher than diatomaceous earth or activated alumina.

Zero Metal Ion Release

Unlike traditional filters, our resin does not release metal ions, preventing secondary oil sludge formation and extending oil life by 3-5 years.

Acidity Reduction Rate 99.8%
Resistivity Improvement > 5.0 GΩ·cm
Particle Removal (ISO 4406) Class 14/11
